A short letter from the studio

We wanted paintings that said something, not just matched the sofa.

Chitra Story began with a simple frustration: most wall art comes with a size and a price, and nothing else. No story, no reason, no reason to remember it in five years.

Every piece on this site is chosen or painted because it carries a story — a place, a memory, a moment — and we write that story down and send it with the painting. If it doesn't have a story worth telling, it doesn't make it to the wall.

— The Chitra Story studio
